About Our Institute

Greenwood Academy is committed to providing quality education in a supportive and inclusive environment. We focus on academic excellence, personal growth, and the development of strong values. Our goal is to prepare students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to succeed in the future.

Our Programs

Primary School (I–V) 📚

A foundation of curiosity, creativity and core skills. Our early years curriculum nurtures the love of learning through play and discovery.

Middle School (VI–VIII) 🔬

Students deepen their understanding across subjects including Science, Mathematics, Languages, Social Studies and Computer Science..

High School – Science 📐

Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics with hands-on lab experience and competitive exam preparation (JEE/NEET)..

High School – Commerce 💼

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics and Mathematics — perfect for future entrepreneurs and finance professionals.

Tech and Art Clubs 🐦‍🔥

Engage in creative and technical activities outside the classroom With Robotics ,coding, Political Science and Fine Arts .
